Economics.....the dismal science par excellence.....Some put all economics in the Hand of God....which is then beyond all human comprehension. Others that all economics is in the hands of mans free will. Surely then we ought to differentiate between what we think ought to be, and what actually is. Perhaps a history of economics is in order.
