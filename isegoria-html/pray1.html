Prayer


So what is prayer? Most Churches seem to assume that everyone knows what is prayer....or that the current leaderships position on prayer is the only one available. In my opinion a good teacher will show the full range of opinion on any subject and leave the student the right to choose.


Luke 10:26 He said unto him, What is written in the law? how readest thou?


For me this means the Creator of the Universe allows each and everyone of us to interpret the Holy Writ according to our own lights. Even a mere male has the right to do this, in spite of the arguments of the ladies. How readest thou? This is permission from the Lord of the Universe.

But the human condition would seem to suggest that each of us wants to rule the world and have our own opinions legislated into law. Very few of us will dare to pray “Thy Will be done.”. To renounce control and let Another take control!Me included of course.
