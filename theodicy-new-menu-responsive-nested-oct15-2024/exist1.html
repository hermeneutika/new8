Philosophy. Of course my Charismatic and Pentecostal friends will think I am in error even thinking about Philosophy. Where is the starting point? If Archimedes said “Give me a lever long enough and I could move the earth”. But so what? What about Aristotle? “The unexamined life is not worth living”. But again so what? Who or what gives Archimedes or Aristotle or anyone else for that matter, the authority and the weight to take on board their statements as absolute truth? For me the question is where may Absolute Truth be found?

The twenty first century solution to this problem seems to be found in pure subjectivism, ie what I choose to believe ,is true, there is therefore no objective standard. But if man indeed is the measure of all things, all and nothing are true.And then even the proposition “Man is the measure of all things” is again subject to SO WHAT? SAYS WHO? AND I HAVNT EVEN STARTED on EMPIRICISM! THE only statements that are true,according to some, are those that can be verified. But the verification principle itself.....how do we know it is absolutely true? What are the criteria for Truth? Maybe Absolute Solipism is the only Truth? But again how is one to know? The Bible itself says


1Co 14:10 There are, it may be, so many kinds of voices in the world, and none of them is without signification.
